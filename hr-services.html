What are HR Services

HR responsibilities include payroll, hiring, firing, and keeping up to date as per the government norms and rules.

A Professional Service provider assumes full responsibility for your company's HR administration. It provides HR end to end solutions to your company's workers by taking full legal responsibility in terms of PF, ESI Subscription of your employees, including having the final say in hiring, firing, and the amount of money employees make. Professional Service Provider handle all the HR aspects and the business handling aspects of the company.... We provide both unskilled and skilled man-power as per the client work and requirement. We conduct background checks and drug testing during the recruitment process We offer assistance in all areas of human resources administration, including recruitment, payroll, regulatory compliance and risk management. Outsourcing human resources functions can reduce your administrative workload and free up your time so you can focus on your business objectives. Managing human resources efficiently and intelligently is critical to your company's success.
